My Speedcubing Story


About Me Rubik's Cube


I was introduced the the rubik's cube on Christmas in 2019 as a present from my mother. I was curious and want to try solve it. Eventually, after 3 months, I finally solved it. It felt amazing to finally solve the puzzle. I instantly wanted to try to solve it faster. Hence, I practised, memorising better algorithms and "drilling" them into my muscle memory.
Speedcubing is one of my favourite hobbies. Currently, I can solve one in around 25 seconds, and less than 20 on a nice scramble. For more information, please check out my YouTube Channel at https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCxKWZyiKYu194Jwz9KZ-DtQ/.
